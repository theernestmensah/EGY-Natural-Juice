Questions

Do I need to keep it refrigerated?

Yes. Every bottle is cold-pressed with no preservatives, so it needs to stay refrigerated (or frozen for some batches) until you're ready to drink it.

What's actually in the juice?

Real fruit, water where needed, and nothing else. Check the label on each can for the exact mix of that flavor.

Do you deliver outside Accra?

Online ordering is on its way. For now, call or message us directly and we'll sort out delivery.

Can I order in bulk for an event?

Yes, reach out and we'll put together a bundle for your event or office.
